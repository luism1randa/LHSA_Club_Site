Fall 2019

You can look forward to some great hands-on activities, medical knowledge, community service, and fundraising events this semester!


Spring 2019

We started our new mentor program where we paired a medical student with each club member, we had a discussion on what to do in the year between graduation and applying to professional school, we networked with healthcare professionals, volunteered in a clinic abroad, learned to suture, and raised money for some great causes!


Fall 2018

We visited the UA College of Medicine, networked with amazing medical students, learned to take vitals, welcomed a representative of the Universidad Autonoma de Guadalajara School of Medicine, helped the Leukemia & Lymphoma society organize the yearly Light the Night Walk, and we even helped build a home!
