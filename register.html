We Welcome Pre-health students of all backgrounds

What's in it for you?

Exclusive Events

We work hard with pre-health advising staff and our connections at the College of Medicine to organize fun, informative, and useful events. Previous events have included: Vitals Clinics, Suture Clinics College of Medicine Tours, Panels with Medical Schools, and more! Take a look at our Events and Gallery pages to see what we've done in the past!

Volunteering

Want to give back to your community but don’t know where to start or don’t want to go alone? ​We organize group events and find the best ways for you to get involved in everything from helping the homeless to building homes for those in need. Just show up and we’ll take care of the rest. Or if you want to volunteer at a hospital we can help with your application!

Mentorship

We've partnered with medical students from the Latino Medical Student Association to create a one-on-one mentorship program. Selected members will be paired with a medical student currently enrolled at the U of A College of Medicine for monthly check-ins, tips, resources, and advice on how to become a successful applicant for medical school.

Resources

The University of Arizona provides access to many opportunities for pre-health students on campus. However we know these are sometimes difficult to find which is why we search for pre-health events, opportunities, ​internships, and resources both on and off campus. Everything is sent to you in an organized newsletter or posted on our social media so you don’t have to go looking yourself.

Pre-Health Advising

Do you need help choosing a major, courses, professional school, building a resume, or applying to programs? Our board members have prior experience with internships and health programs and are here to give advice. Our amazing pre-health advisors are here to guide you along every step of the way. And our friendly medical students can provide their first-hand experiences.

Network

As an undergraduate in pre-health you'll quickly learn that networking is an essential part of your career. Being well connected to faculty, health professionals, and other students will make it much easier to find resources and achieve your goals. We have the contacts and provide a central location to make it easy for you to connect with likeminded individuals and make new friends.
